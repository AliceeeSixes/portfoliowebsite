Become the Storm
itch.io youtube playlist

"Become the Storm" is a game I worked on as part of the Brackey's 2024.2 Game Jam

I wrote the music for this soundtrack in two main styles - rock and electronic.
The gameplay has two distinct phases to it, so I made them feel separate by giving two different musical sounds.

Planning phase

The music for the planning phase was quiet, tense electronic music.
I made custom synths for this piece to create the atmosphere I wanted.


Combat phase

To contrast this, the combat phase music is high-energy rock, using recorded
electric guitars played by myself. This piece draws on some of my pop-punk influences.


The main menu theme, and the win stinger are based on the combat music, and the loss stinger uses
a synth I made for the planning music.
