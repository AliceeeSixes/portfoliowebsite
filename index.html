About me

Hi! My name is Alice Stiles

I am a freelance composer, specialising in music for video games.
I can compose orchestral, electronic, rock, retro, or any combination of
those styles.

I have worked on a few game jams lately and I am now looking to do paid
work writing music for your games!

I have a degree in Music and Technology from the University of Liverpool,
and I have been writing music for almost a decade. My favourite games
include Skyrim, Counterstrike and Minecraft.

I also have an interest in programming and web development,
which includes making this website from scratch in VSCode. If you have
any feedback on the website be sure to let me know!
